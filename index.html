Welcome! I am a self taught former educator turned Software Engineer. I am passionate about creating quality projects and love the challenge of learning new and exciting languages in tech. My interests include coffee, code and cats. Reach out to work with me today!

My Tech Story

After teaching for five years, I decided to pursue other ventures. Tech had always piqued my interest, but it wasn't until I spoke to a student about helping them reach their goals that a spark from my past ignited. I remembered countless hours spent tinkering with HTML and creating websites in my youth. I could still feel the thrill of figuring out the right {tag} to add an image or link to a favorite website. Inspired by this rediscovery, I began listening to the Code Newbies podcast. It was there I realized I wasn't alone. Many others had settled into careers that didn't excite them, often for reasons of practicality or uncertainty. But for me, figuring things out is a passion. It's why I love puzzles and why coding resonates so deeply. It's not just about writing code; it's about solving problems, finding elegant solutions, and piecing together the puzzle of how things work. And that's what I'm determined to pursue.
